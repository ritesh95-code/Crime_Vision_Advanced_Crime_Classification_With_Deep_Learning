This is an introduction to the Crime Classification project.

Types of crime to be detected

Assault: Threat of violence that puts someone in fear of harm
Explosion: A violent outward burst of energy, often accompanied by sound and light
Fighting: Physical conflict between two or more people
Shooting: Discharging a firearm
Stealing: Taking something that doesn't belong to you without permission
Abuse is a broader term that encompasses other acts on your list, like assault and battery.
Arrest is a consequence, not a crime itself.
Arson is the criminal act of deliberately setting fire to property.
Burglary is the crime of entering a building illegally to commit a crime, typically theft.
Robbery is stealing by force or threat of force.
Shoplifting is stealing from a retail store.
Vandalism is the act of deliberately damaging or destroying property.
NormalVideos and RoadAccidents are not crimes.
